IoT and Robotics Engineering

Where paper talks to silicon

Think of IoT as the world's digital nervous system — billions of sensors sending whispers through the cloud.
Think of robotics as the muscles and reflexes — machines that reach, roll, and react.
IoT + Robotics Engineering is teaching those muscles
to listen to the nervous system, so a farm knows when to water itself, a hospital cart finds its own way, and a factory builds without lights.
